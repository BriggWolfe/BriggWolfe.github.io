The Three Disciplines

🏊
Swim

The race begins in the water. Endurance, technique, and breathing efficiency are everything in the open water swim.

🚴
Bike

The longest leg of the race. Power output, aerodynamics, and smart pacing determine how much you have left for the run.

🏃
Run

The final test. Running on tired legs after swimming and cycling separates those who trained smart from those who didn't.
