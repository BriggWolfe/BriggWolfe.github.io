What is a Triathlon?

A triathlon is a multi-sport endurance race consisting of three continuous legs: swimming, cycling, and running. Athletes transition between each discipline as fast as possible, and the clock never stops. It is one of the most demanding tests of physical and mental toughness in all of sport.

Race Distances

  1. Sprint Triathlon
    • Swim: 750 meters
    • Bike: 20 kilometers
    • Run: 5 kilometers
  2. Olympic Triathlon
    • Swim: 1,500 meters
    • Bike: 40 kilometers
    • Run: 10 kilometers
  3. Half Ironman (70.3)
    • Swim: 1.9 kilometers
    • Bike: 90 kilometers
    • Run: 21.1 kilometers
  4. Full Ironman
    • Swim: 3.86 kilometers
    • Bike: 180 kilometers
    • Run: 42.2 kilometers
